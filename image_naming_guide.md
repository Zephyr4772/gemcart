# Product Image Naming Guide

## Naming Convention
- Basic products: `num-(jewelry abbreviated).jpg`
- Category-specific products: `num-(jewelry abbreviated)-(category abbreviated).jpg`

## Abbreviations
- Ring: rng
- Necklace: nck
- Earring: erg
- Bracelet: brl
- Watch: wch
- Gold: g
- Silver: s
- Platinum: p
